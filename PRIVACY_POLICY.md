# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2026

## Introduction

TanStack Wonderland ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: We collect information about how you interact with our application, including pages visited, features used, and time spent.
- **Technical Data**: We collect technical information such as browser type, device type, operating system, and IP address.
- **Performance Data**: We collect performance metrics to improve application speed and reliability.

### Information You Provide
- **Pokemon Teams**: Teams you create are stored locally in your browser's localStorage.
- **Preferences**: Language preferences and UI settings are stored locally.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our service
- Improve user experience
- Monitor application performance
- Detect and prevent errors
- Analyze usage patterns

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your Pokemon teams and preferences are stored locally in your browser.
- **No Server Storage**: We do not store your personal data on our servers.
- **Third-Party APIs**: We use PokeAPI for Pokemon data, which may collect standard web server logs.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

## Third-Party Services

- **PokeAPI**: We use PokeAPI to fetch Pokemon data. Please review their privacy policy.
- **Vercel**: Our application is hosted on Vercel. Please review their privacy policy.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data stored locally
- Delete your data (clear browser localStorage)
- Opt-out of analytics tracking (disable in browser settings)

## Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking for:
- Performance monitoring
- Error tracking
- Analytics (if enabled)

You can disable tracking through your browser settings.

## Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights:
- Right to access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

## CCPA Compliance

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Access your personal information
- Request deletion of personal information

## Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Local storage data persists until you clear your browser data.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:
- HTTPS encryption
- Security headers (CSP, HSTS)
- Input validation and sanitization
- Rate limiting
